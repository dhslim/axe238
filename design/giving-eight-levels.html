God Loves a Cheerful Giver · 2 Corinthians 9:7

The Eight Levels of Giving

"The Lord loves a cheerful giver."

There is a ladder to charity. Each rung gives more truly than the one below it — from the reluctant gift at the bottom to the gift that frees a person from need altogether at the top.

The highest rung — toward the open door
1
The Highest

He who gives, and also assists the recipient in addressing the reason for the need in the first place.

2

He who gives without knowing who will receive — and without the recipient knowing who gave it.

3

He who gives and knows who will receive it — but the recipient will not know who gave it.

4

He who gives and does not know who receives — but the recipient knows who gave it.

5

He who gives enough, and before he is asked — but with both parties knowing each other.

6

He who gives enough — but only after he is asked.

7

He who does not give enough — but what he gives, he gives graciously.

8
The Lowest

He who does not give enough — and even that, unwillingly.
