**The Ox — the work of God.** The ox is perennial strength: the laboring power of the ministry.

## Spiritual purpose in the house of God

- A **champion in the work of the ministry** — building, planting, harvesting, teaching, reaching, and delivering souls and saints. *(Jeremiah 1:10)*
- Demonstrates the **work ethic of Jesus Christ**. *(Isaiah 61:1–3)*
- Works alongside the saints to **encourage and prosper** them in their own ministry. *(Ephesians 4:11–12)*
