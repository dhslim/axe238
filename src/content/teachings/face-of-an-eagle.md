**The Golden Eagle — spiritual apex predator, the lord of the skies.** An eagle can spot prey from over three miles away and dive at more than 200 miles per hour.

## Spiritual purpose in the house of God

- **Spiritual sight** — detecting allies and adversaries before they become obvious. *(Isaiah 62:6; Ezekiel 3:17; Hebrews 13:17)*
- The capacity to **operate in the power of the light (the Sun)** — the strength of God — shielded in both ascent and descent. *(Malachi 4:2–3; 1 Timothy 6:16; Revelation 1:16)*
- Confronting enemies that appear far greater **through the power of God**. *(1 Samuel 17:37, 45–47)*
