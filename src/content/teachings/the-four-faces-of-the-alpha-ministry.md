**The revelation of Jesus Christ.** Before the throne are seven lamps of fire — the seven Spirits of God — and four living creatures, full of eyes before and behind: the first like a **lion**, the second like a **calf (ox)**, the third with the face of a **man**, and the fourth like a flying **eagle**. Each cries, “Holy, holy, holy, Lord God Almighty.” *(Revelation 4:5–11)*

These four faces are the characteristics of the Alpha preacher:

- **Lion** — righteousness, boldness, and protection
- **Ox** — the work of God
- **Man** — spiritual order
- **Eagle** — spiritual sight
