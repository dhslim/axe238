**The key of the house of David.** “And the key of the house of David will I lay upon his shoulder; so he shall open, and none shall shut; and he shall shut, and none shall open.” *(Isaiah 22:22; cf. Revelation 3:7)*

This is the **Key of David**, made available to the Philadelphia church in the end time. Two figures stand as emblems:

- **Eliakim** — the emblem of **Philadelphia**, given the key and the government upon his shoulder.
- **Shebna** — the emblem of **Laodicea**, removed from his station.

*The original teaching is built around an interactive mind-map; a static, accessible version is planned for the rebuild.*
