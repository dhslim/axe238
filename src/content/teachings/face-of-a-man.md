**Man — the Face of God.** The man embodies spiritual order.

## Spiritual purpose in the house of God

- **Wields the divine sword** in judgment against evil. *(Romans 13:1–4)*
- **Rules as God** — “as a prince hast thou power.” *(Genesis 32:28)*
- **Bears the brokenness of Christ** in ministry, to deliver souls. *(Isaiah 53:3–11)*
- Is **taught by God** for spiritual warfare. *(Exodus 15:3; Psalm 144:1; 2 Corinthians 10:3–5)*
